📄 About Us – GyanBox

Welcome to GyanBox!

GyanBox is a free educational platform created especially for Class 9 and 10 students. Our goal is to bring together the best educational resources available on YouTube — all in one place — to make learning simple and accessible.

🎯 Our Purpose

We understand that finding quality study materials online can be time-consuming. That’s why GyanBox was created — to save your time and guide you to the best video lectures and notes available online.

📚 What We Do

  • We collect and organize the best free YouTube video lectures by expert teachers.
  • We provide easy-to-understand notes based on these videos.
  • We do not create original content — we simply curate the best publicly available content to help students.

⚖ Transparency

All the content featured on our platform is freely available on YouTube. We always credit the original creators and do not claim any ownership of the video content.

💡 Our Vision

Our vision is to make high-quality education accessible to every student, especially those who cannot afford tuition or coaching classes. We want to make learning easy, stress-free, and completely free.

🙏 Thank You

Thank you for using GyanBox. We hope it helps you learn better and achieve great success in your studies!

Let’s Learn. Let’s Grow. Together.
